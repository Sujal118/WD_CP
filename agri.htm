Empowering Farmers, Enriching Nation

Welcome to the Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture and improve the welfare of farmers across the nation. We provide various schemes and services to support the agricultural community.

About Us

The Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is responsible for agricultural development, farmer welfare programs, and implementing policies aimed at achieving sustainable growth and food security. Our mission is to improve rural livelihoods and support innovations in farming techniques.

Our Schemes

  • Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY): Crop insurance to protect farmers against unforeseen losses.
  • Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY): Irrigation solutions for improved water resource management.
  • Soil Health Card Scheme: Enhancing soil productivity with detailed soil analysis and recommendations.
